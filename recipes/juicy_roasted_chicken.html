Juicy Roasted Chicken

Description

This chicken recipe is a classic. It's comforting, delicious, and simple.

This rustic dish is perfect for an easy night at home.

Tell us if you enjoyed the recipe!

Ingredients

  • Three pound whole chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Butter
  • Celery
  1. Season the chicken inside and out
  2. Add butter to the chicken inside and out
  3. Stuff the cavity with celery
  4. Bake until the chicken is fully roasted
